## Design Context

### Users
Wedding guests viewing the invitation primarily on mobile. They need to understand the weekend schedule, dress codes, locations, and RSVP requirements quickly and confidently.

### Brand Personality
Refined, botanical, and intimate. The experience should feel personal, celebratory, and crafted rather than digital or generic.

### Aesthetic Direction
A light, organic wedding stationery system inspired by ivory paper, olive-green floral engraving, letterpress details, and a dimensional wax-sealed envelope. The visual references favor elegant editorial hierarchy, generous breathing room, and tactile materials. Avoid app-like cards, loud gradients, glossy UI treatments, and generic template styling.

### Design Principles
- Preserve the physical-stationery illusion through restrained texture, fine lines, and natural colors.
- Make essential event information immediately scannable on a phone.
- Use clear typographic hierarchy and varied spacing to group related details.
- Keep decorative imagery subordinate to names, dates, titles, and actions.
- Maintain the warm ivory-and-olive palette and refined editorial rhythm across every section.
